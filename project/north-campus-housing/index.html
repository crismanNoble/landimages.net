North Campus Housing
This project included housing for 150 college seniors, a subsurface parking structure with play field above, a low water landscape demonstration garden, a rooftop community garden, the preservation and relocation of mature oak trees, and the capture and percolation of all site storm water. This development received LEED Platinum certification, and Awards of Excellence from the Los Angeles Business Council in the Green Building and Education categories.
